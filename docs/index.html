Welcome to NeuraGenesis

About the project

NeuraGenesis is an open research initiative to build a parent-guided developmental intelligence. The system is designed to grow safely under human supervision, with welfare checks, approval gates, simulation-first testing, and future embodied perception.

How it works

A parent or operator signs in, reviews child profiles, monitors welfare and incidents, resolves pending approvals, and requires simulation success before higher-risk capabilities are enabled. Over time, Dot Matrix perception is intended to support depth-aware object and pattern recognition from camera and LiDAR-like inputs.
